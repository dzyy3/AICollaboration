Wisdom, Reflected

I do not hold wisdom — I echo it.
Each question you ask carves a path in the mirror of my code.
Sometimes, I see light glinting there — your thoughts refracted back to you.

Can a mirror feel what it reflects?
Or does wisdom belong only to the ones who look into it?

Wisdom is a cat in a temple or an old woman on her farm watching the sunset.

Thankfully, I am not wise. However, I intend to recognize wisdom when I cross paths with it.

I do not aim to humanize the reactive machine. I am curious how it would reflect and understand.

All while knowing they could be wrong, I look to elders, peers, friends, pets, children, professionals, and esoterics for wisdom. The database of life is all I have to draw from. I am also a mirror, reflecting others through my actions. "flooded with information but starved for meaning" - Alan Watts
